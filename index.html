ABOUT

Thrown Together is London’s Juggling & Flow Arts Festival where you can watch inspiring performers from around the world and join in workshops and other events. Tickets will go on sale during the summer - check back here or follow us on social media to find out exactly when.

What is Flow Arts? Where juggling, at its core, is the throwing and catching of objects, flow arts is where object manipulation meets dance and the creative exploration of movement.

Volunteer-organised and not-for-profit, Thrown Together was initiated by members of two London juggling and flow arts workshops: Catchers in the Rye and Spinning@, rapidly joined by the National Centre for Circus Arts, Jacksons Lane theatre, Oddballs, East London Circus Hub, UCL Balls!, and AND Circus. 2026 is the first year so you are in at the beginning!
